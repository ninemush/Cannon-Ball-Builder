Core product goal

CannonBall’s goal is to produce, every single time, a:

Studio-stable package
with as much real business logic preserved as possible
with localized TODO placeholders only where necessary
and an honest, complete, actionable Developer Handoff Guide

CannonBall is not trying to be a rigid compiler that blocks whenever uncertainty exists.

CannonBall accepts that AI plus rules-based logic will not cover 100% of real-world scenarios. That is expected. The fallback is:

preserve the workflow and business intent wherever possible
complete the 80% CannonBall can do well
localize the unresolved portion to the smallest possible scope
mark those gaps clearly with TODO - Update this with XXX
record them precisely in the DHG with specific developer actions

This 80/20 outcome is still massively valuable and is the real product target.

Critical clarification: do NOT reinterpret this as “block package generation”

I do not want the pipeline to become more rigid or fail-fast by default.

I am not saying package generation should be blocked whenever CannonBall cannot fully complete all business logic.

What I am saying is:

activity-level incompleteness is acceptable
package-level structural instability is not acceptable

In other words:

CannonBall may degrade business logic locally
CannonBall may insert activity-level TODO placeholders
CannonBall may produce degraded or stubbed activities with clear handoff guidance
but CannonBall must not deliver a package that fails to open in Studio or opens with broad structural/package-authoring defects

So the correct philosophy is:

Prefer localized degradation over global failure.
Prefer preserving executable business logic over rigid blocking.
Prefer honest TODO handoff over silent logic loss.
Hard fail only when the package is not Studio-stable/openable, the artifact cannot be trusted, or there is a known high-confidence silent runtime corruption that cannot be localized into a developer-actionable handoff.

Non-negotiable package quality standard

CannonBall may degrade unresolved business logic at the smallest possible activity scope with explicit TODO placeholders and DHG guidance, but it must not deliver packages with foundational Studio-authoring defects.

Packages must:

open in UiPath Studio every single time
be free of broad structural errors
be free of missing variables that break authoring
be free of broken/orphaned flows that make the package unstable
be free of invalid or inconsistent arguments that make the package noisy/broken in Studio
be free of missing namespace declarations / broken xmlns usage
be free of malformed XML/XAML
be free of missing essential files like Main.xaml
be free of missing dependencies or other foundational package defects that make Studio load unreliable

Acceptable fallback:

localized activity-level stubs or degraded activities
explicit TODO placeholders
TODO text that describes the missing business logic clearly
TODO text that specifies the exact developer action required
matching DHG entries with exact edit guidance

Unacceptable output:

package fails to open in Studio
package opens with tons of structural/authoring errors
package-wide instability is used as the fallback mechanism
