- unresolved workflow references
- unknown or invalid activity schema
- malformed project.json
- manifest/package parity failure
- hardcoded credentials/secrets
- output that is not Studio-openable

Please treat these as warnings/non-blocking when the package is otherwise openable:
- incomplete business logic that can be finished by a developer
- safe stubbed workflows
- conservative simplifications in baseline_openable mode
- non-critical implementation gaps that are clearly documented in the handoff guide
