CORRECTION 2 — Score normalisation
The confidence score must be normalised, not 
capped. Use this formula:

  score = triggeredSignalWeights / totalPossibleWeight

Where totalPossibleWeight is the sum of all signal 
weights (approximately 1.7 based on the spec). 
This means a score of 1.0 only occurs if every 
single signal fires. Two moderate signals produces 
a proportional score, not a maxed-out one. Keep 
the AUTO_ENGAGE_THRESHOLD at 0.6 — adjust after 
seeing real scores in production.

CORRECTION 3 — Per-workflow validation
Replace the 8K global token cap with per-workflow 
validation calls. For each workflow file:
  - Make one Haiku call with that workflow's XAML 
    plus its declared variables
  - Cap each call at 6K input tokens (single 
    workflow rarely exceeds this)
  - Merge the CorrectionSet results across all 
    calls before applying
  
This costs slightly more per generation but gives 
complete coverage. For a 9-workflow process, expect 
9 small Haiku calls rather than 1 truncated call. 
At Haiku pricing this is still very cheap.
